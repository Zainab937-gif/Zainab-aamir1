## First Day at University
![uni](/assets/UET-Faisalabad-Campus.jpg){: width="150"}
My first day at university was a memorable experience, as I went there with my parents. Their presence gave me comfort, but I still felt nervous because it was a completely new environment.

Senior students guided us about classes and departments, which helped reduce confusion. During the first two days, we attended orientation sessions where teachers introduced themselves and motivated us. These sessions helped me feel more confident and prepared.

---

## First PF Lab Experience
![Lab](/assets/image.jpg){: width="150"}

In my first Programming Fundamentals (PF) lab, I felt both excited and anxious. We learned how to create and delete folders using CMD and GUI.

Initially, I found it difficult, especially using CMD, but with practice and guidance, I improved. This experience boosted my confidence and helped me become more comfortable with technical tasks.

---

## Python Learning & Quizzes

We started learning Python with basic concepts like input/output. It was interesting but challenging.

There was confusion due to different theory and lab instructors. My first quiz result was low, but I worked hard and improved, eventually achieving full marks in the next quiz.

---

## Mid-Semester Challenges

In labs, we were not allowed to use mobile phones or AI tools. This helped us develop independent problem-solving skills.

Initially, my quiz marks were low, but with consistent practice, I improved and scored **38/40 in midterms**, which was a big achievement.

---

## Machine Learning Challenges

We learned machine learning in theory but did not practice it in labs, which made it difficult.

Other sections had better practical exposure, which made me feel at a disadvantage. This created stress, especially because our project was based on machine learning.

---

## Machine Learning Project

We developed a **heart attack prediction system** using machine learning.

Due to my partner’s absence, preparation was difficult. However, after rescheduling, we managed to prepare and complete everything successfully.

---

## Project Exhibition

The exhibition was one of the best experiences. Teachers and HOD visited our stall and appreciated our work.

This boosted my confidence and improved my presentation and teamwork skills.

---

## A Memorable Day

During the final presentation, technical issues and electricity problems occurred. Despite this, I answered all questions confidently.

We used the **Decision Tree algorithm** and prepared a detailed report. This project significantly improved my understanding of machine learning.

---

## Improving My Coding Skills

One lab experience restricted the use of laptops and internet. This forced us to rely on our own knowledge.

I learned that understanding fundamentals is very important before using tools like ChatGPT.

---

## Final Lab Experience

The final week was stressful with multiple submissions and evaluations.

Despite technical issues and power outages, I completed all tasks successfully and achieved full marks.

---

## Final Exams Result

During finals, I became ill, which affected my preparation. However, I still achieved an **A grade in Programming Fundamentals**, which made me proud.

---

## Second Semester – Database Course

In the second semester, Sir Bilal taught both theory and lab, which improved consistency.

We learned databases and created a website using WordPress in lab sessions.

---

## Database Learning

We studied MySQL, Primary Key, and Foreign Key concepts.

We also learned to create ER diagrams, which improved our understanding of database design.

---

## Online Classes & GitHub Portfolio

Due to external conditions, classes shifted online. Managing studies during Ramadan became challenging.

We were assigned to:
- Work on a machine learning model  
- Create a GitHub portfolio  

This helped improve both technical and professional skills.

---

## Conclusion

My university journey has been challenging but rewarding. I learned the importance of:
- Consistency  
- Hard work  
- Self-learning  

I am continuously improving and working towards my goals.
