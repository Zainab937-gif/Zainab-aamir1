## First Day at University
![uni](/assets/UET-Faisalabad-Campus.jpg){: width="150"}


---
My first day at university was both exciting and nervous. I went to the university with my parents because everything was new for me. When I arrived on campus, I saw many students, teachers, and senior students guiding newcomers. Although I felt a little uncomfortable in the beginning, the welcoming environment helped me settle down.

During the first two days, we attended orientation sessions where teachers introduced themselves and explained university life. They encouraged us to stay focused, work hard, and make the most of our academic journey. These sessions helped me understand the expectations of university studies and reduced much of my nervousness.

A few days later, I checked my timetable and learned that Dr. Bilal Ahmad would be teaching some of our courses. At that time, I did not know much about his academic background, but I later discovered that he has expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. His practical approach to teaching made learning more interesting and connected classroom concepts with real-world applications.

As the semester progressed, I explored the research work of Dr. Bilal Ahmad through his <a href="https://scholar.google.com.au/citations?user=8nZ0jVkAAAAJ&hl=en">Google Scholar profile</a>. It was inspiring to see how machine learning can be applied to solve important real-world problems, especially in the healthcare domain.

Looking back, my first day at university marked the beginning of an important chapter in my life. It introduced me to a new learning environment, new opportunities, and teachers who motivated me to think beyond textbooks. The experience taught me that every academic journey starts with a single step, and that first step was truly memorable.
