The final examination period was one of the most challenging times of my first semester. After completing assignments, projects, lab evaluations, and presentations, it was finally time to appear in the final exams. Unfortunately, during this important period, I became ill, which made studying and preparing for exams much more difficult.

As a day scholar, I travel a considerable distance between my home and the university. Normally, I use public transport, but because of my health condition, my father personally dropped me off and picked me up from the university during the examination days. His support helped me focus on my exams despite feeling unwell, and I am truly grateful for his care and encouragement.

Due to my illness and the heavy workload of the last few weeks, I was unable to revise some of the topics properly. This made me worried about my performance because I knew I could have prepared better under normal circumstances. However, instead of giving up, I decided to trust the hard work I had done throughout the semester.

One thing that gave me confidence was my preparation in Programming Fundamentals. Throughout the semester, I had spent time practicing coding, completing lab tasks, and improving my understanding of programming concepts. Those efforts helped me during the final examinations, even when I could not revise everything perfectly.

Dr. Bilal Ahmad always encouraged students to focus on continuous learning rather than depending entirely on last-minute preparation. His guidance helped me understand that consistent effort throughout the semester is more valuable than studying only before exams. I also explored his professional achievements through his <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/drbilalphd/">LinkedIn profile</a>, which motivated me to remain committed to my academic goals.

When the results were announced, I was extremely happy to learn that I had achieved an A Grade in Programming Fundamentals. Despite all the challenges, health issues, and stress, my hard work had paid off. This achievement boosted my confidence and reminded me that determination and consistency can lead to success even in difficult situations.
